Math Formula Guide

BetterLearn supports LaTeX math formulas in your flashcards. This guide shows you how to write common mathematical expressions.

Basic Syntax

To write a math formula, place it between dollar signs:

  • For inline formulas: $your formula here$
  • For display formulas (centered): $$your formula here$$
